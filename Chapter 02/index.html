Plot

"Inception" follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from people's minds during their dreams. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased in exchange for planting an idea into a target's subconscious — a process called inception.

Key Information

  • Director: Christopher Nolan
  • Release Year: 2010
  • Genre: Science Fiction, Action, Thriller
  • Duration: 148 minutes
